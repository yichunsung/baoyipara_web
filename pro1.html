稻穀專業乾燥機

Grain Dryers

從設計、製造、現場施工裝機、試機等一貫作業是交鑰鎖工廠的做法
同時也要尋找各國家的代理，我們會教授、訓練
培訓你們成為獨立、專業的乾燥機代理商。
20 幾年來工廠以乾燥機的製造為主，員工有 100 多人
產品銷售到台灣、中國、泰國、越南、柬埔寨、緬甸、非洲、土耳其等國家

乾燥機一天 (24H) 可以乾燥量：從 6 噸到 150 頓
乾燥機熱源：粗糠、木材、玉米稈、煤、柴油及煤氣等
可乾燥穀物：稻穀、豆類等。

From the designing, manufacturing, on-site constructions to testing.
We will provide all the procedures above to customers.
We are also looking for distributors in different countries,
we will teach and train to help you become a independent agent.
The factory has been manufacturing grain dryers for over 20 years, having more than 100 employees,
exporting to Taiwan, China, Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, Myanmar, Africa and Turkey…etc.

Drying from 6 tons ~ 150 tons per day (24 hrs)
Fueling by Rice Husks, Wood, Corn Stalks, Coal and Diesel…etc.
Able of drying Rice, Corn and Beans.
