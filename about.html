ABOUT US

寶宜百敦公司自 2009 年開始將台灣的農、工業精品引進到中國及東南亞國家，幾年下來獲得很好的成果並受到相關行業的認同，同時也改善了當地人民的經濟及生活。本公司於台灣的合作對象包括中華民國外交部、財團法人國際合作基金會、經濟部工業局、資訊工業策進會、台灣省體育總會、DHL 等。而我們所代理行銷的產品都有專業生產技術及研發人才做後盾，讓客戶無後顧之憂，在各國均有實際成功的例子。我們定意要開發更多的精品並行銷到全世界，讓更多的人有便利舒適的生活。

我們的產品皆有原廠品質的保證、原廠技術支援，我們亦可 OEM 您的廠牌或是使您成為原廠代理商。本公司亦在 2018 年加入台灣農機同業公會，並多次參與國際推廣、參訪等事宜，若您有好的產品需要推廣，我們可以幫您帶到全世界有需要的地方。


Bao Yi Paraminton Corp. has been exporting Taiwan’s finest agricultural and industrial products to China and Southeast Asian countries since 2009. Over the years, we have achieved excellent results and gained recognition from related industries, contributing to economic growth and improving living standards.

Our company has collaborated with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, International Cooperation and Development Fund, Industrial Development Bureau, Institute for Information Industry, Taiwan Sports Federation, DHL, and more. All our products are backed by professional manufacturing technology and R&D support, ensuring reliability for our customers. We also have successful case studies across multiple countries. Committed to sourcing the best products and introducing them to the world, we strive to enhance people's lives.

With guaranteed quality and technical support from original manufacturers, we offer OEM services for your brand or can help you become an authorized agent of original brands. Our company became a member of TAMMA Taiwan in 2018 and has been working with global partners ever since. If you have high-quality products that deserve global exposure, let us help you bring them to the world.
