並擁有 SGS, HACCP, USDA等認證
一切都是採用天然種植而非基因改造

Our rice is certified by SGS, HACCP, and USDA, ensuring the highest quality standards.

All of our rice is naturally cultivated and Non-GMO.

Our Low-GI White Rice offers the same great taste as ordinary white rice, but without the high sugar and calorie burdens.

It is the ideal choice for individuals with diabetes or cardiovascular conditions, providing a healthier alternative to traditional white rice.
