有機檸檬

Organice Lemons

台灣生產，由配合農科廠研發合適培養液
並以高黏性土壤綜合胺基酸肥全程採有機種植
檸檬已種植約 2 年，種植面積約 2.5 公頃，年產量約 12 公噸
栽培品種以優利卡 (Eureka) 為主，俗名為「四季檸檬」
果實較其他檸檬小、又多汁，富含檸檬酸等有機酸、維生素C與纖維
果皮含精油是其芳香之來源。

Grown in cultivation fluid with high-viscosity soil, our Eureka lemons thrive in optimal conditions.

Our planting area covers approximately 2.5 hectares, yielding 12 tons annually.
The Eureka variety, cultivated in Taiwan and commonly known as the "All-Season Lemon," is rich in citric acid, other organic acids, vitamin C, and fiber.

Its peel contains essential oils, which give it a distinct aroma and fragrance.
